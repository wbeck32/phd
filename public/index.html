Products and Services
Voice Recordings
Smaller Size Project
Preserve the voice of your loved one forever. Our expert interviewer comes to your home and draws out the stories that make up a life. These are then lightly edited and preserved on private online storage and on a USB card in an attractive presentation box.
Reminiscence Books
Reminiscence Books Medium Size Project
Our signature product is the mid-length reminiscence book for downsizers. It is geared towards those who are letting go of a beloved home or some of their belongings. Our interview team comes to your home to create a photographic record of cherished items and to record the stories behind each one, which are then combined into a beautifully designed book.
In-depth Histories
In-Depth Histories Larger Size Project
Long form books can take several months to more than year to complete, but they are worth the time and investment. These complex histories trace a family’s roots and give current and future generations a detailed portrait of decades and generations past. They tell the story of lives worth remembering.

Why create your personal history?
Why Create Your Personal History? Have you been meaning to write down your family history or personal story? Is it time to capture an elder’s stories before it is too late? These stories are important, whether your fame extends around the block or across the world. A professionally produced personal history brings the past to life and preserves the vitality of family stories for generations to come. We’re here to help. Call or e-mail for a free consultation.
How we work with you
How We Work With You Through interviews and photographs, we create legacy books and recordings for you. Our husband and wife team brings a wealth of project experience and care to every book and recording we create. We provide design, writing, editing, photography and photo restoration, genealogy and historical research services as needed to create a legacy that you can keep and share. We start with a free consultation to understand what your goals are. We follow with a proposal and then we work with you to produce a book or recording that ensures future generations will hear the stories that you care about.

About Miles and Leora
